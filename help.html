Frequently Asked Questions

Go to Route List at the top of the page or go to your Favourites. Then, find the route that you would like to view. Click View Live Cam.

Go to Route List at the top of the page. Then, find the route that you would like to add to your Favourites. Click on View Live Cam. On the route details page, click the star in the top right corner. The route has now been added to your Favourites.

Click Home at the top of the page. Your Favourite routes will appear on your Home page once you have favourited a route.
